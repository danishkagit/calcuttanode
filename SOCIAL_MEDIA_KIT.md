## 1. CANVA DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Brand Colors (Use in Every Graphic)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Background Dark | `#171A26` | Slide backgrounds |
| Surface Dark | `#1E2235` | Card backgrounds |
| Neon Cyan | `#00E5FF` | Headings, accents, CTAs |
| Electric Violet | `#8B5CF6` | Secondary headings, borders |
| Text Primary | `#F1F5F9` | Body text |
| Text Muted | `#94A3B8` | Subtitles, labels |
| Gradient | `#00E5FF → #8B5CF6` | Buttons, hero text |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Small text on dark |

### Fonts
- **Headings:** Poppins Bold or Montserrat Bold
- **Subheadings:** Poppins SemiBold
- **Body:** Inter Regular or Nunito Regular
- **Prices:** Poppins Bold, size 150% of body

### Global Settings
- **Canvas size:** 1080 × 1080 px (square for feed)
- **Reels:** 1080 × 1920 px (9:16)
- **Corner radius on cards:** 16px
- **Border on cards:** 1px `#8B5CF6` at 20% opacity, hover becomes `#00E5FF` at 40%
- **Glow effect:** Duplicate text layer, blur 15px, opacity 30%, color cyan
- **Gradient text:** Use "Linear gradient" fill, angle 135°, `#00E5FF` → `#8B5CF6`
