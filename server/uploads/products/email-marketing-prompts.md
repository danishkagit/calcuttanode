# Email Marketing Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Marketing

Build high-converting email funnels, from welcome sequences to re-engagement campaigns.

---

1. **Welcome Sequence Architect**
   "Create a 4-email welcome sequence for {product/service}. Email 1 (Day 0): 'Welcome + Set Expectations' — thank you, what to expect, what to do first. Email 2 (Day 1): 'Deliver Value' — best blog post/resource. Email 3 (Day 3): 'Build Relationship' — founder story or behind-the-scenes. Email 4 (Day 5): 'First Offer' — introductory discount or free consultation CTA."

2. **Subject Line Tester**
   "Write 20 subject lines for an email about {topic/offer}. Categories: 5 curiosity-gap, 5 benefit-driven, 5 urgency/FOMO, 5 personalized. For each: character count, spam-assess score (1-5, 5=most likely to trigger spam filters), and predicted open rate (low/medium/high) based on {industry} benchmarks."

3. **Newsletter Content Generator**
   "Write a weekly newsletter edition for {brand} about {theme}. Sections: Editor's Note (2 sentences, personal), Main Content (200 words with one key insight/tip/trend), Quick Links (3 curated resources with one-line descriptions), Community Spotlight (1 shoutout or testimonial), and CTA (one action, low friction). Tone: {brand voice}."

4. **Abandoned Cart Flow Builder**
   "Design a 3-email abandoned cart sequence for {product} at {price}. Email 1 (1 hour): Friendly reminder + product benefits recap + social proof (review snippet). Email 2 (24 hours): Objection handling — address top 3 hesitations with FAQs + live chat invite. Email 3 (72 hours): Last chance — limited-time incentive (free shipping/10% off/bonus item) + scarcity timer."

5. **Re-engagement Campaign Writer**
   "Write a 2-email re-engagement sequence for subscribers inactive for {days}+ days. Email 1: 'We miss you' — remind them why they joined, share something new/exciting, ask for feedback with a 1-click survey (why did you stop engaging?). Email 2: 'Last chance' — offer a 'come back' incentive + preference center link to update frequency. If no response, tag for sunset."

6. **Promotional Email Copywriter**
   "Write a promotional email for {offer} launching on {date}. Pre-header: complements subject line, under 100 chars. Body: Hook (problem/aspiration) → Agitation (cost of inaction) → Solution (product with 3 key benefits) → Social proof (1 testimonial or metric) → CTA (clear, single button). Include urgency element (limited stock/time)."

7. **Email Sequence Copy Doctor**
   "Diagnose and improve this email sequence: '{paste sequence}'. Check: subject line → preview text alignment, hook strength in first 2 sentences, value-to-promotion ratio (aim 80:20 for nurture, 50:50 for sales), CTA clarity and prominence, mobile readability (font size, button size, line length), and unsub/reason trigger placement."

8. **Event Registration Funnel**
   "Create a 5-email sequence for {event type} happening on {date}. Email 1 (2 weeks before): Announcement + early bird pricing. Email 2 (1 week before): Speaker lineup or agenda highlights. Email 3 (3 days before): Last early bird + social proof (who's attending). Email 4 (1 day before): Final reminder + logistics. Email 5 (day after): Replay/recap + next event teaser."

9. **List Segmentation Strategy**
   "Design a segmentation strategy for an email list of {size} subscribers for {business}. Segments: engagement level (active/dormant/cold), purchase behavior (bought/never bought/recency), content preference (tutorials/case studies/product updates), demographics (industry/role/company size), and lead source. For each segment: recommended send frequency, content type, and re-engagement trigger."

10. **Transactional Email Upgrader**
    "Rewrite these transactional emails for {business} to include marketing/upsell opportunities while maintaining their functional purpose: '{current transactional emails}'. For each: verify the critical info is prominent, add a relevant but non-distracting recommendation or CTA below the fold, and ensure brand voice consistency."

11. **A/B Test Designer**
    "Design 5 A/B test ideas for improving email campaign performance for {business}. For each: variable to test (subject line, preview text, sender name, CTA button color/copy, hero image vs no image), hypothesis, minimum sample size per variant, success metric (open rate, CTR, conversion), and duration (based on send volume)."

12. **Drip Campaign Educator**
    "Write a 7-email educational drip campaign for {complex product/service}. Email 1-2: Problem education (why the status quo is costly). Email 3-4: Solution introduction (how your approach is different). Email 5-6: Implementation guide (how to get started + what to expect). Email 7: Case study + consultation CTA. Each email: 150-200 words, one clear concept, one actionable takeaway."

13. **Holiday/Event Campaign Calendar**
    "Create an email campaign calendar for {business} for the next 3 months ({months}). For each month: 1-2 campaign ideas with seasonal/event hooks, send schedule, target segment, offer strategy, content requirements, and success metrics. Include: one evergreen campaign that runs continuously regardless of season."

14. **Feedback & Survey Email Writer**
    "Write an email requesting feedback after {customer action}. Types: post-purchase satisfaction (NPS survey), post-support interaction (CSAT survey), post-event/webinar feedback, product feedback request, and annual relationship survey. Each: personalized subject line, clear time commitment, incentive if applicable, and a genuine 'we'll use this' statement."

15. **Email Deliverability Optimizer**
    "Audit this email sending setup for deliverability issues: {sender details}. Check: SPF/DKIM/DMARC records, sending domain reputation, list hygiene practices, engagement-based sending strategy, spam complaint rate estimation, and authentication setup. Provide specific fixes for each issue found, prioritized by impact on deliverability."
