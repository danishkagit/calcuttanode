# Productivity & Life Hacks Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Productivity

Master your time, energy, and focus with AI-powered productivity systems.

---

1. **Time Audit Analyzer**
   "Analyze my current time allocation: '{description of typical week}'. Identify: time drains (activities with low ROI), peak energy windows, multitasking patterns to eliminate, and scheduling gaps. Suggest a redesigned weekly schedule using time-blocking with: deep work blocks (90 min), shallow work batches (30 min), recovery breaks (15 min), and buffer time (10% of day)."

2. **GTD System Architect**
   "Design a Getting Things Done (GTD) workflow for a {profession}. Cover the 5 stages: Capture (inbox system for {tools}), Clarify (decision tree for next actions), Organize (project lists, someday/maybe, reference), Reflect (weekly review checklist with 10 items), and Engage (context-based task selection criteria)."

3. **Pomodoro Technique Optimizer**
   "Create a customized Pomodoro protocol for {type of work}. Determine: ideal focus duration (25/45/90 min based on task complexity), break type (micro 3min/standard 7min/long 20min), session structure (4 pomodoros → long break), and interruption management strategy. Provide a daily schedule template for {hours} hours of focused work."

4. **Decision Matrix Builder**
   "I'm deciding between {option A} and {option B} regarding {decision context}. Build a weighted decision matrix. Criteria: {list 5-7 criteria}. Weight each criterion by importance (1-10). Score each option (1-10). Calculate weighted scores. Include: a 'regret test' for the losing option and a 'sleep test' question to check gut feel."

5. **Habit Stacking Planner**
   "Design a habit stacking routine for {goal}. Use the formula: 'After [current habit], I will [new habit].' Create: morning routine (4 habits stacked), workday routine (3 habits), evening routine (3 habits). For each habit: specify the cue, the routine, the reward, and the minimum viable version (2-minute rule) for low-motivation days."

6. **Meeting Efficiency Consultant**
   "Transform this recurring meeting: '{meeting description}'. Apply: clear agenda (sent 24h before), time limit (shorten by 25%), async-first approach (what can be decided via doc), RACI framework for decisions, meeting roles (facilitator, timekeeper, note-taker), and action-item capture format. Target: reduce meeting time by 40% while improving outcomes."

7. **Energy Management Tracker**
   "Create an energy management system for {chronotype} (morning/night person). Design a daily schedule that aligns tasks with energy levels: peak hours → creative/deep work, medium hours → collaborative/meetings, low hours → admin/email, recovery → exercise/rest. Include: 3 energy-renewing micro-breaks, meal timing strategy, and sleep optimization checklist."

8. **Workspace Ergonomics Auditor**
   "Audit my workspace setup: '{description}'. Check: monitor height/ distance, chair ergonomics (90-90-90 rule), keyboard placement, lighting (natural vs artificial), noise management, air quality, and digital hygiene (desktop organization, notification settings). Provide a prioritized fix list (quick wins today vs investments for next month)."

9. **Weekly Review Prompt**
   "Guide me through a 30-minute weekly review. Part 1 (5 min): Celebrate 3 wins. Part 2 (10 min): What didn't get done and why? Part 3 (10 min): Preview next week — top 3 priorities, potential blockers, resource needs. Part 4 (5 min): One system adjustment to try next week. Output: a clear 'next week commit' list with 3 must-dos and 5 should-dos."

10. **Digital Declutter Specialist**
    "Design a 7-day digital declutter plan. Day 1: Phone home screen audit (remove 10 apps). Day 2: Email cleanup (unsubscribe from 20 lists + 0-inbox sprint). Day 3: Desktop/file organization (folder structure). Day 4: Notification overhaul (allow-list only). Day 5: Social media diet (curate follows + time limits). Day 6: Password manager setup. Day 7: Maintenance systems (weekly 15-min digital tidy protocol)."

11. **Learning Sprint Designer**
    "Create a 30-day learning plan for {skill/topic} with 30 minutes/day. Week 1: Foundation (core concepts + vocabulary). Week 2: Application (build first project/practice piece). Week 3: Deep dive (advanced technique + troubleshooting). Week 4: Integration (teach someone else + real project). For each week: specific resource links, practice exercise, and success metric."

12. **Focus Mode Environment Creator**
    "Design an optimal focus environment for {type of work}. Elements: sound (binaural beats/lo-fi/nature/ silence specific), lighting (color temperature, brightness), digital tools (distraction-blocking app config), physical tools (what's on desk, what's hidden), ritual (5-minute pre-focus routine), and environment switch cues (different location for different work types)."

13. **Bias & Blind Spot Checker**
    "I'm making a decision about: '{decision}'. Help me identify potential cognitive biases. Check for: confirmation bias (seeking evidence for preferred choice), anchoring (first info influence), sunk cost (past investment influence), status quo bias (preference for current state), overconfidence (accuracy of predictions). For each: provide a debiasing technique + specific question to ask myself."

14. **Travel & Logistics Planner**
    "Create a travel optimization plan for {trip details}. Pre-trip: packing checklist (customizable template), document organization (digital + physical copies), itinerary sharing protocol. During trip: daily rhythm suggestion, backup plans for common disruptions (lost luggage, delayed flight), expense tracking system. Post-trip: recovery day protocol, expense reconciliation, trip debrief questions."

15. **End-of-Day Reflection Prompt**
    "Design a 10-minute end-of-day reflection ritual. Journal prompts (3): What went well today? What would I do differently? What's one thing I'm grateful for? Review: did I honor my priorities? Check: what's my top priority tomorrow? Close: shut-down ritual (clean desk, review calendar, set intention). Output: a 3-sentence daily log entry with a tomorrow-first-task identified."
