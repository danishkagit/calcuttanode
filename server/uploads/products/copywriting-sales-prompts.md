# Copywriting & Sales Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Marketing

Write persuasive copy that converts browsers into buyers with these battle-tested sales prompts.

---

1. **AIDA Sales Letter Generator**
   "Write a complete sales letter using the AIDA framework for {product/service}. Attention: bold headline + hook. Interest: 2-3 pain points with storytelling. Desire: features → benefits → social proof. Action: urgent CTA with guarantee. Target audience: {audience description}."

2. **Value Proposition Refiner**
   "Rewrite this value proposition to be clearer and more compelling: '{current VP}'. Apply the '3-second test' — a visitor should understand the offer in 3 seconds. Use the formula: 'We help [audience] [achieve outcome] by [unique mechanism]'."

3. **Email Sequence Writer**
   "Write a 5-email nurture sequence for {offer}. Email 1: Hook + problem awareness. Email 2: Story/credibility. Email 3: Solution deep-dive. Email 4: Social proof + FAQs. Email 5: Urgent close + guarantee. Each email under 200 words, including subject line and preview text."

4. **Landing Page Copy Architect**
   "Structure the copy for a landing page selling {product/service}. Sections: above-fold headline + subhead + CTA, 3 benefit columns, how-it-works (3 steps), social proof (3 testimonials + logos), objection handling (FAQ), and final CTA with urgency. Write the first 2 sections."

5. **Objection Handler**
   "A prospect says: '{common objection}'. Write 3 different responses — logical (data-driven), emotional (story-driven), and social-proof-driven. Each response: 50-75 words, ends with a micro-commitment question to keep the conversation moving forward."

6. **Social Proof Collator**
   "Transform these raw testimonials into polished social proof: '{raw testimonial text}'. For each: create a headline version (1 line), a quote version (3-4 sentences), a case study micro-version (problem → solution → result with numbers), and a 5-star review format."

7. **CTO/Technical Decision Maker Email**
   "Write a cold email to a CTO/Technical Director about '{product/service}'. Tone: technical but not overwhelming. Include: specific technical pain point, how your solution fits their stack, ROI calculation with assumptions, and a low-friction next step (15-min screen share, not a demo)."

8. **Price Justification Script**
   "Create a price justification script for a {price} product in the {industry} market. Compare against: cost of not solving the problem, competitor pricing (if more expensive → feature advantage; if cheaper → value efficiency), and long-term ROI with a 12-month projection."

9. **Brand Voice Guide Creator**
   "Create a brand voice guide for {company name} in the {industry} space. Define: brand archetype, tone spectrum (formal/casual), vocabulary (words to use/avoid), sentence structure preference, and 3 sample rewrites of a neutral sentence showing the brand voice applied."

10. **Product Launch Sequence**
    "Write a 7-part product launch sequence for '{product name}'. Day 1-2: Tease the problem. Day 3-4: Educational content building up to solution. Day 5: Product reveal + early bird offer. Day 6: Live demo + Q&A. Day 7: Last chance + bonus stack reveal. Each: subject line + 3-sentence body + CTA."

11. **FAQ Content Writer**
    "Write 10 FAQ entries for {product/service}. Each Q&A: question phrased as the customer would ask, answer in 40-60 words with one social proof element or statistic, and a micro-CTA to the next logical page. Cover: pricing, setup time, compatibility, support, and cancellation."

12. **Case Study Framework**
    "Outline a 4-section case study for {client name} who achieved {result} using {product/service}. Section 1: The Challenge (before state with metrics). Section 2: The Solution (implementation timeline). Section 3: The Results (before/after comparison). Section 4: The Verdict (client quote + recommendation)."

13. **Video Sales Script (VSL) Writer**
    "Write a 3-minute video sales script for {offer}. 0:00-0:30: Hook + problem. 0:30-1:00: Agitate the pain. 1:00-1:45: Present solution with 3 key benefits. 1:45-2:15: Social proof + testimonials. 2:15-2:45: Objection handling. 2:45-3:00: Urgent CTA with guarantee."

14. **Upsell/Cross-sell Copy Generator**
    "Write upsell copy for offering '{upsell product}' after a customer purchases '{original product}'. Positioning: enhancement, not separate purchase. Use 'You bought X — here's what Y does that X doesn't' framework. Price anchoring: show original price vs bundle price."

15. **Cart Abandonment Recovery**
    "Write a 3-email cart abandonment sequence for {product} at {price}. Email 1 (1 hour after): Friendly reminder + social proof. Email 2 (24 hours): Objection handling + testimonial addressing top hesitation. Email 3 (72 hours): Last chance with limited bonus or small discount. All under 100 words."
