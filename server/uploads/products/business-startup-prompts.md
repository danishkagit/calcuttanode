# Business & Startup Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Business

Validate ideas, write pitch decks, and scale your startup with AI-powered business strategy prompts.

---

1. **Business Model Canvas Generator**
   "Fill out a Business Model Canvas for {business idea}. Complete all 9 blocks: Value Proposition, Customer Segments, Channels, Customer Relationships, Revenue Streams, Key Resources, Key Activities, Key Partnerships, and Cost Structure. Highlight the riskiest assumption in each block."

2. **Lean Canvas Validator**
   "Create a Lean Canvas for {idea}. Focus on: Problem (top 3), Solution, Key Metrics, Unique Value Proposition, Unfair Advantage, Channels, Customer Segments, Cost Structure, and Revenue Streams. For each block, rate your confidence level (1-5) and suggest one experiment to validate or invalidate it."

3. **Pitch Deck Outline Creator**
   "Structure a 10-slide pitch deck for {company/idea}. Slide 1: Problem (with relatable story). Slide 2: Solution (product screenshot or demo). Slide 3: Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM with sources). Slide 4: Business Model. Slide 5: Traction (metrics graph). Slide 6: Competition (2x2 matrix). Slide 7: Team. Slide 8: Financial Projections. Slide 9: Ask. Provide headline and key data point for each slide."

4. **Investor Email Writer**
   "Write a cold email to an angel investor/VC firm interested in {sector}. First paragraph: Personalized hook referencing their portfolio or recent investment. Second: One-line pitch + key traction metric. Third: Specific ask ($ amount for % equity) + what the funds will achieve. Fourth: Low-friction CTA (15-min call). Max 150 words."

5. **Market Research Analyst**
   "Analyze the market for {product/idea}. Provide: market size (TAM/SAM/SOM with sources), 5 key competitors with their market share/%/pricing, 3 emerging trends in the next 12 months, target customer persona (demographics + psychographics), and regulatory/barrier considerations for {country}."

6. **Pricing Strategy Consultant**
   "Recommend a pricing strategy for {product/service} targeting {customer segment}. Current cost: ${cost}. Competitor prices: {list}. Analyze: value-based vs cost-plus vs competitor-based pricing, suggest 3 pricing tiers with specific feature differentiation, and recommend a launch promotion strategy. Include a price sensitivity test framework."

7. **Customer Discovery Interview Script**
   "Write a 30-minute customer discovery interview script for validating {problem hypothesis}. Sections: Warm-up (2 min) → Problem exploration (10 min with 5 open-ended questions) → Solution validation (10 min with 3 concept tests) → Willingness to pay (5 min) → Wrap-up (3 min). Include go-questions vs show-questions guidance."

8. **Growth Experiment Designer**
   "Design 5 growth experiments for {business} currently at {stage}. For each: hypothesis statement, experiment design (control vs variant), success metric with target threshold, sample size needed, duration, cost, and go/no-go decision criteria. Prioritize by ICE score (Impact, Confidence, Ease)."

9. **Financial Projection Builder**
   "Create a 3-year financial projection for {business type}. Include: monthly revenue forecast (Year 1), quarterly (Years 2-3), COGS breakdown, operating expenses by category, headcount plan with salary costs, cash flow statement, break-even analysis, and 3 scenarios (base/best/worst). Assumptions list required."

10. **OKR & KPI Framework Creator**
    "Design an OKR framework for a {industry} company with {team size} employees. Company-level OKRs (3 objectives with 3 key results each). Department-level OKRs for: Product, Marketing, Sales, Operations. Define 10 key metrics to track weekly with target thresholds. Include an OKR scoring rubric."

11. **Partnership Proposal Writer**
    "Write a partnership proposal for {company A} to partner with {company B}. Opportunity: specific mutual benefit. Proposed structure: revenue share %, responsibilities split, duration, and exit clauses. Include: market opportunity sizing, integration complexity estimate, and a draft MOU outline."

12. **Hiring Job Description Optimizer**
    "Write a compelling job description for {role} at a {stage} startup. Structure: About Us (2 sentences, mission-driven), Role Overview (3 sentences), Responsibilities (5-7 bullets), Requirements (5-7 bullets), Nice-to-Haves (3-5 bullets), Culture & Benefits (3 bullets), and a non-generic 'Why Join Us' section. Max 400 words."

13. **Product Roadmap Prioritizer**
    "Given these features: {list features}, prioritize them using RICE scoring (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort). For each: assign scores 1-10 for each RICE dimension, calculate final RICE score, plot on a 2x2 impact-vs-effort matrix, and recommend a phased roadmap (Now/Next/Later buckets)."

14. **Competitive Battle Card Creator**
    "Create a sales battle card for competing against {competitor name} in the {industry} space. Sections: Their strengths (and how to neutralize), their weaknesses (and how to exploit), comparison table (feature-by-feature), common objections they'll raise + your responses, and winning positioning statement (15 words)."

15. **Founder Burnout Prevention Plan**
    "Create a sustainable weekly schedule for a solo founder/early-stage startup founder. Include: focused work blocks (4 x 90 min), meeting-free days (2/week), exercise/sleep schedule, delegation opportunities, decision fatigue reduction strategies, and monthly review process with specific wellness check-in questions."
