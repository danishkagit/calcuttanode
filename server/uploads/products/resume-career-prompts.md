# Resume & Career Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Career

Land more interviews with ATS-optimized resumes, compelling cover letters, and strategic career navigation prompts.

---

1. **ATS-Optimized Resume Builder**
   "Create an ATS-optimized resume for a {job title} with {years} years of experience. Industry: {industry}. Key skills: {list}. Use: standard section headers, keyword density matching the job description below, quantifiable achievements with {metric} format, and no graphics/tables. Target job description: '{paste JD}'."

2. **Cover Letter Generator**
   "Write a tailored cover letter for {job title} at {company name}. Opening: Hook with a specific achievement relevant to their recent {company news/project}. Body: 3 bullet points connecting your experience to their job requirements. Closing: Confident CTA + follow-up mention. Max 300 words."

3. **LinkedIn Profile Optimizer**
   "Rewrite my LinkedIn profile for {target role}. Optimize: headline (120 chars with keywords), about section (3 paragraphs: who I help → how I do it → proof + CTA), experience section (3 bullet points per role with metrics), and featured section recommendations. Current profile: '{summary}'."

4. **Behavioral Interview Prep**
   "Generate 10 STAR-method answers for common behavioral questions for a {job title} role. Questions: teamwork, leadership, failure, conflict, adaptability, achievement, pressure, innovation, mistake, and decision-making. Each: Situation (2 lines), Task (1 line), Action (4 lines), Result (with metric)."

5. **Salary Negotiation Script**
   "Write a salary negotiation script for a {job title} offer at {company}. Current offer: ${amount}. Market range: ${min}-${max}. Include: opening email/script to HR, talking points for the call, responses to common pushbacks ('budget constraints', 'we're at the max'), and timing strategy."

6. **Career Transition Roadmap**
   "Create a 90-day roadmap to transition from {current role} to {target role}. Week 1-4: Skill gap analysis + learning resources. Week 5-8: Portfolio projects + networking targets. Week 9-12: Application strategy + interview prep. Include specific milestones and success metrics for each phase."

7. **Portfolio Project Pitch**
   "Write a compelling case study for my portfolio project: '{project description}'. Structure: Problem (2 lines) → Process (4 lines with methodology) → Solution (3 lines) → Results (with metrics) → My Role (tech stack + responsibilities). Write in first person. 250 words max."

8. **Networking Message Templates**
   "Write 5 LinkedIn DM templates for networking with {target audience}. Types: informational interview request (cold), post-application connection (warm), conference follow-up (warm), alumni connection (warm), and recruiter outreach (cold). Each under 100 words with a clear, low-friction ask."

9. **Skill Gap Analyzer**
   "Compare my current skills: {list skills} against the requirements for {target role}. Identify: top 3 skill gaps with the highest ROI to fill, free/paid resources to close each gap (with time estimate), and how to credibly demonstrate each new skill (certification, project, or portfolio piece)."

10. **Resume Bullet Point Transformer**
    "Transform these weak resume bullet points into powerful, metric-driven statements: '{weak bullets}'. Rules: start with action verb, include {metric} format (%, $, time saved), tie to business impact, remove passive voice, max 2 lines per bullet. Show both original and improved versions."

11. **Job Search Tracker Prompt**
    "Create a job search tracking system with columns for: company, role, date applied, outreach contacts, interview stage, follow-up dates, and notes. For each of the next 5 application targets: research the company, find a mutual connection, customize the application, and set follow-up reminders."

12. **Freelance Profile Builder**
    "Write an Upwork/Fiverr profile for {service offering}. Title: keyword-optimized and specific. Overview: problem-agitate-solution format with client-focused language. Portfolio description: 3 case studies with problem → approach → result. Packages: 3-tier pricing with clear scope boundaries."

13. **Thank You Note Generator**
    "Write a post-interview thank you email for {job title} at {company}. Reference: a specific topic discussed in the interview (mention it), re-emphasize your fit for the role, address a concern they raised (if any), and include a call to action about next steps. Send within 4 hours of interview."

14. **Personal Brand Statement Creator**
    "Create 3 personal brand statement options for a {job title} specializing in {niche}. Format: 'I help [audience] [achieve outcome] through [unique method]'. Each option: one sentence (Twitter bio length), two sentences (LinkedIn headline length), and a paragraph (About section length)."

15. **Performance Review Self-Assessment**
    "Write a self-assessment for my {time period} performance review as a {job title}. Structure: top 3 achievements (with metrics), areas of growth (honest but framed positively), skills learned, goals for next period, and specific support/resources needed from management. Tone: confident but humble."
