# SEO & Content Marketing Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Marketing

Unlock higher rankings and engaging content with these SEO-optimized prompts for Google, YouTube, and social media.

---

1. **Blog Post Outline Generator**
   "Create a detailed blog post outline for '{topic}' targeting the keyword '{keyword}'. Include H2-H4 headings, FAQ schema ideas, internal linking suggestions, and a meta description under 160 characters. Target search intent: {informational/commercial/transactional}."

2. **SEO Meta Description Writer**
   "Write 5 click-optimized meta descriptions for '{page title}' targeting '{primary keyword}'. Each under 160 characters, include a number or power word, a CTA, and the target keyword naturally. Specify which one works best for {search intent}."

3. **Keyword Clustering Tool Prompt**
   "Group the following keywords into topical clusters for a {industry} website: {list of 10-15 keywords}. For each cluster, suggest a pillar page topic and 3-5 supporting blog post ideas. Include internal linking structure recommendations."

4. **YouTube SEO Optimizer**
   "Optimize this YouTube video title and description for search: '{current title}'. Suggest 5 alternative titles using high-volume keywords, a 200+ word description with timestamps, 15 relevant tags, and a thumbnail text idea that increases CTR."

5. **Local SEO Content Generator**
   "Write a 500-word Google Business Profile post for a {business type} in {city}. Include local keywords, mention 3 nearby landmarks, a special offer, and a CTA. Also suggest 5 FAQ questions with answers for the GBP Q&A section."

6. ** Featured Snippet Optimizer**
   "Rewrite this paragraph to target a 'People also ask' featured snippet for the query '{question}'. Structure the answer in 40-50 words, use a bulleted or numbered list format, and include '{target keyword}' naturally in the first sentence."

7. **SEO-Friendly Product Description**
   "Write a {word count}-word product description for '{product name}' that includes '{primary keyword}' in the first 100 words, has 3 benefit-focused subheadings, a comparison table, and a FAQ section with 3 schema-ready Q&A pairs."

8. **Link Building Outreach Email**
   "Write a personalized cold email template to request a backlink from {website URL}. Reference a specific article on their site, explain why our resource '{resource title}' adds value to their readers, and keep it under 150 words. Include subject line options."

9. **Content Gap Analysis Prompt**
   "Based on these competitor URLs: {list 3 competitor URLs}, identify 10 content gaps for a {industry} website. For each gap, suggest a unique angle, target keyword with monthly search volume, and content format (listicle, guide, video, infographic)."

10. **SEO Audit Summary Generator**
    "Create a 5-point executive summary of this SEO audit for {domain}. Cover: technical issues found, top 3 on-page optimizations, backlink opportunities, competitor gaps, and quick wins that can be implemented in 48 hours. Tone: professional but urgent."

11. **Schema Markup Content Helper**
    "Generate FAQPage schema-structured content for 8 frequently asked questions about {topic}. Each answer should be 30-60 words, include a related long-tail keyword, and be formatted for copy-paste into a WordPress FAQ block or JSON-LD."

12. **Social Media SEO Caption Writer**
    "Write 10 social media captions for {platform} promoting '{blog post title}'. Each caption: under 150 characters, includes 2-3 high-volume hashtags, teases the value prop, and ends with a CTA. Platform-specific tone: {professional/casual/funny}."

13. **E-E-A-T Content Booster**
    "Rewrite this section to better demonstrate Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness: '{original text}'. Add: a personal experience anecdote, a data point with source, an author bio snippet, and external citations from authoritative domains."

14. **Alt Text & Image SEO Prompt**
    "Write descriptive, keyword-rich alt text for {number} images related to '{topic}'. Each alt text: 5-12 words, includes a long-tail keyword variant naturally, describes what the image shows, and avoids keyword stuffing. Also suggest optimized file names."

15. **SEO Newsletter Content Pack**
    "Create a 4-part email newsletter series around the theme '{theme}'. Each email: subject line under 50 chars, preview text under 100 chars, 200-300 words body, 1 CTA button, and 3 internal links. Include send day recommendations and A/B test subject line variants."
