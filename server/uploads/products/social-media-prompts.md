# Social Media Management Prompt Pack
**15 PROMPTS** | Category: Marketing

Dominate every platform with scroll-stopping content for Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, and Facebook.

---

1. **30-Day Content Calendar**
   "Create a 30-day social media content calendar for {brand/niche}. Include: 4 posts per week across {platforms}, content themes for each week, holiday/event tie-ins for {month}, post type variety (educational, entertaining, promotional, UGC), and optimal posting times."

2. **Instagram Caption Writer**
   "Write 10 Instagram captions for {topic/theme}. Each caption: hook in first line, 100-150 words, 8-12 relevant hashtags (mix of 100k, 1M, and 10M+ buckets), emoji strategy (2-3 per caption), and a conversation-starting question in the last sentence."

3. **LinkedIn Thought Leadership Post**
   "Write a LinkedIn post about {industry insight/opinion}. Format: bold ONE-liner hook, 3-paragraph body with a contrarian take + data point, line breaks for readability, 5 relevant hashtags, and a poll or question to drive comments. Tone: authoritative but approachable."

4. **Twitter/X Thread Writer**
   "Write a 10-tweet thread about {topic}. Tweet 1: Hook that stops the scroll. Tweets 2-8: Value-dense content with one idea per tweet. Tweet 9: Story/personal anecdote. Tweet 10: CTA + lead magnet. Each tweet under 240 characters, with thread numbering."

5. **TikTok Script Generator**
   "Write a 30-60 second TikTok script for {topic}. Hook in first 3 seconds, pattern interrupt (text overlay, zoom, or sound effect timing), value delivery in 15 seconds, engagement loop in last 5 seconds (comment/save/share CTA). Include on-screen text cues."

6. **Hashtag Strategy Builder**
   "Build a 3-tier hashtag strategy for {niche} on {platform}. Tier 1 (30%): Broad hashtags with 10M+ posts. Tier 2 (40%): Medium hashtags with 500K-1M posts. Tier 3 (30%): Niche-specific hashtags with <100K posts. Total: 15-25 hashtags. Explain why each tier matters."

7. **Bio & Profile Optimizer**
   "Write 5 options for a {platform} bio for {brand/person}. Each: under {character limit} chars, communicates unique value proposition in first line, includes relevant keywords for search, has a clear CTA + link strategy, and matches brand voice: {tone}."

8. **Ad Copy Generator**
   "Write 5 Facebook/Instagram ad copy variants for {product/service}. Each: primary text under 125 chars, headline under 40 chars, description under 30 chars. Include: 2 benefit-driven, 1 social-proof-driven, 1 urgency-driven, 1 curiosity-gap variant. Specify best visual type for each."

9. **Community Engagement Script**
   "Write 10 comment templates for engaging with {niche} accounts. Types: value-add comment (adds information), question comment (drives conversation), compliment + question (relationship building), and supportive comment. Each under 50 words and not generic."

10. **Analytics Report Explainer**
    "Translate this social media analytics data into a plain-English report: '{raw data}'. Explain: what each metric means, whether it's good/bad for {industry}, top 3 actionable insights, and recommended next steps. Include: engagement rate, reach, follower growth, and conversion data."

11. **Reel/TikTok Trend Adaptor**
    "Take this trending audio/format: '{trend description}' and adapt it for {brand/niche}. Preserve the core structure but change the content to be relevant to {industry}. Describe: visual scenes, text overlays, timing, and why this adaptation would resonate with {target audience}."

12. **Influencer Collaboration Brief**
    "Write a collaboration outreach message to an influencer with {follower count} followers in {niche}. Offer: {compensation type}. Campaign goal: {goal}. Include: brand intro (2 sentences), collaboration idea (3 sentences), what they get, and a low-pressure CTA. Keep under 200 words."

13. **Content Repurposing Map**
    "Create a repurposing plan for this long-form content: '{content title}'. Map it to: 5 LinkedIn posts, 3 Twitter threads, 1 Instagram carousel (10 slides), 2 short-form videos (TikTok/Reels), 1 email newsletter, and 1 podcast episode outline. Show how each format changes the angle."

14. **Crisis Management Response**
    "Draft a crisis response social media post for {scenario}. Type: {apology/clarification/update}. Include: acknowledgment of the issue (within first 2 lines), what happened, what we're doing about it, timeline for resolution, and where to get updates. Tone: transparent, accountable, solution-focused."

15. **Engagement Pod / Commenting Strategy**
    "Create a 7-day commenting strategy for growing a {niche} account. For each day: list 5 target accounts to engage with, specific comment ideas (not generic), DM outreach template for meaningful connections, and a 'content snippet' to post that day that encourages discussion."
